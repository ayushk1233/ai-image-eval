# 🚀 AI Image Evaluation Platform: Full User Walkthrough

Welcome to the **AI Image Evaluation Platform**! This tool is designed to benchmark the latest state-of-the-art generative AI image models (GPT-5 Mini, Gemini 2.5 Flash, and Gemini 3.1 Flash) specifically for their performance on Indian E-commerce Fashion photography.

This guide provides a complete walkthrough of how to navigate the application, what information you can extract, and what actions you can take.

---

## 1. 🏠 Home (Registration & Login)
When you first open the application, you will land on the **Home** page. Because this platform conducts scientifically rigorous human evaluations, we need to track raters.
- **Action:** Enter your **Name**, **Email**, and **Age** in the sidebar.
- **Flow:** Click "Register/Login". The application will securely store your participant profile and issue a session cookie. You are now ready to evaluate or generate images!

---

## 2. 🎨 Generate (Creating New Data)
Once logged in, navigate to the **Generate** tab on the left sidebar. This is where you can interact directly with the AI models.

### What you can do:
- **Use Benchmark Prompts:** Select from a dropdown of **20 highly-curated pre-loaded prompts** designed to test complex Indian aesthetics (e.g., "Navratri chaniya choli", "Kanjeevaram saree"). 
- **Use Custom Prompts:** Toggle to the "Custom Prompt" option to type your own specific e-commerce use-case. *(Note: Custom prompts are isolated to your user session and are not saved to the global benchmark dataset).*
- **Generate:** Click the **Generate Images** button. The system will asynchronously call all three models (GPT-5 Mini, Gemini 2.5, Gemini 3.1) at the exact same time.
- **View Outputs:** The generated images will render side-by-side on your screen so you can immediately see how the models interpreted the prompt differently.

---

## 3. ⚖️ Evaluate (The Core Workflow)
The **Evaluate** tab is the heart of the platform. This is where the blind human evaluation occurs.

### What happens here:
- **Blind A/B Testing:** You are served a random, unrated image generated by one of the models. You will **not** be told which model generated the image (to eliminate brand bias).
- **The Rating Scale:** You will see a detailed form with 10 sliders, allowing you to rate the image on a 1-5 Likert scale across highly specific dimensions:
  1. Prompt Adherence
  2. Visual Quality
  3. Indian Cultural Relevance
  4. Commercial Viability
  5. Product Focus
  6. Anatomical Correctness
  7. Lighting Consistency
  8. Fabric & Texture Realism
  9. Demographic Authenticity
  10. Overall Impression *(Located at the very bottom)*
- **Submit:** Click "Submit Rating". Your scores are saved to the database, and the next unrated image is instantly queued up for you.

---

## 4. 📜 History (Your Personal Log)
The **History** tab acts as your personal ledger for everything you've done on the platform.

### What information you get:
- **Evaluated Images:** See a complete gallery of images you have already rated, along with the scores you gave them.
- **Unrated Generations:** If you generated images in the "Generate" tab but haven't rated them yet, they will appear here.
- **Actionable Steps:** For any unrated images, the UI provides a direct option for you to submit a rating right from the history tab. Once rated, the UI will update to proudly display: *"Rating submitted thanks, your score matters."*

---

## 5. 📊 Dashboard (Real-Time Analytics)
The **Dashboard** tab is the command center for data analysts and researchers. It aggregates every rating submitted by every user across the platform in real-time.

### What information you get:
- **High-Level Stats:** See the total number of images evaluated (meaning ratings submitted), the total number of participants, and the current overall leading model.
- **Radar Charts:** An interactive, full-width radar chart plots the average score of all 3 models across all 10 rating criteria. You can easily spot which model excels at "Fabric Realism" versus "Product Focus".
- **Detailed Metrics Table:** A clean, interactive data table that breaks down the exact average score for every single metric for every model, alongside their "Win Rate".
- **Benchmark Report Graphs:** Static, high-fidelity visualizations exported from the `reports/` folder, including the "Overall Score" and the crucial "Focus vs Realism" matrix.
- **Export Data:** Click the **Download CSV** button to download the raw data. The export safely strips out all user PII (Name, Email, Age) while maintaining all the prompts, model names, and the 10 metric scores for your own external analysis.

---

## 6. 🔬 Full Report (Deep Dive Research)
For stakeholders who want the final verdict without digging through the raw data, the **Full Report** tab offers an editorial, "Josh Talks Research" style whitepaper.

### What information you get:
- **The Commercial Gap:** A detailed explanation of why aggregate scores are misleading, and how GPT-5 Mini dominates "Product Focus" while Gemini dominates "Fabric Realism".
- **API Economics:** Interactive bar charts detailing the Unit Economics of the models, breaking down the **Average Latency (seconds)** and **Cost per 100 images** based on OpenRouter industry estimates.
- **Visual Evidence:** Side-by-side visual galleries of actual generated images (e.g., Men's Festive Kurta, Bridal Lehenga) complete with their human-rated scores to physically demonstrate the platform's findings.
